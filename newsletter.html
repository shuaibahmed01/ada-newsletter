ADA comments on proposed Medicare payment policies for dental services in 2025

Author: Olivia Anderson

Published: 2024-09-12 00:00:00

The ADA submitted comments to CMS on proposed Medicare payment policies for dental services in 2025, marking the third consecutive year of such input. The ADA supports coverage for dental services related to end-stage renal disease and dialysis, while requesting clarification on diabetes-related dental care. The association also suggested delaying implementation of certain administrative modifiers and ICD-10 coding requirements until 2026, and proposed adopting a standardized referral form for care coordination.

House committee holds 50th ERISA anniversary hearing

Author: Olivia Anderson

Published: 2024-09-13 00:00:00

The House subcommittee on Health, Employment, Labor and Pensions held a hearing on September 10, 2024, to discuss ERISA's 50th anniversary and potential improvements. The ADA submitted comments emphasizing the importance of state authority in regulating dental benefits. The hearing focused on protecting private insurance and avoiding a single-payer system, with experts debating ERISA's impact on employer-sponsored health benefits.

Symposium to highlight orthodontic innovation, technology

Author: Mary Beth Versaci

Published: 2024-09-17 00:00:00

The International Orthodontics Foundation and ADA Forsyth Institute are hosting their first joint symposium on orthodontic innovation and technology on October 5-6, 2024. The event features 30 speakers from 24 institutions, including ADA Executive Director Raymond A. Cohlmia, D.D.S. Virtual attendance options are available, with paid CE credits or free registration for IOF members.
